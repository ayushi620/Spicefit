Healthy Eating & Home Remedies

Tips from moms, grandmothers, and elders to stay healthy, fight illnesses, and eat wisely.

Cold & Flu

Drink warm ginger-honey tea to soothe the throat and boost immunity.

— Mom

Eye Health

Include carrots and leafy greens in your diet for stronger eyesight.

— Dadi

Digestion

Eat light meals, include fennel seeds or cumin water to aid digestion.

— Nani

Immune Boost

Include seasonal fruits, citrus, and nuts in your diet to strengthen immunity.

— Mom

Skin Glow

Eat antioxidant-rich foods like berries and turmeric for healthy skin.

— Dadi

Stomach Ache

Warm water with a pinch of ajwain or ginger can relieve bloating and gas.

— Nani

Healthy Weight

Eat whole grains and balanced meals; avoid excessive fried foods.

— Mom

Hydration

Drink water with lemon or herbal infusions to stay hydrated and active.

— Dadi

Liver & Detox

Eat leafy greens, bitter gourd, and avoid processed foods for liver health.

— Mom
